Data cleaning instructions

  • Place the mouse over flagged (orange) points to view flag information.
  • Click and drag across any plot to bring up the options menu.
  • To flag a series of points, choose a label in the dropdown menu, optionally add a comment, and click either the orange or red button:
    • Orange: flag all selected points.
    • Red: flag only red points (identified by the outlier detector as potentially anomalous).
  • To remove flags, select flagged points and press the green button.
  • To zoom back to the full extent, double-click any plot.
  • To move the time window, click "Previous four weeks" or "Next four weeks", or choose a time range from the dropdown.
